MH Corporate Basic By MH Themes

Version 1.3.6 – MH Corporate basic WordPress Theme is a clean, modern and fully responsive free business WordPress theme with a light and minimalistic styling, which is perfectly suited for corporate and business websites. This flexible business WordPress theme is ideal to promote your products and services or to run a corporate blog. By using the widgetized homepage template, you are also able to build a magazine-like layout including a slider and display your posts on the front page sorted by categories.
