SKT Pathway By SKT Themes

Version 1.6 – SKT Pathway is a responsive mobile friendly multipurpose WordPress theme and can be used for interior design, medical, health, fitness, corporate, business, consulting, real estate and news, blog, portfolio type of websites. Since it is compatible with portfolio slider, gallery plugins and WooCommerce for online shop and contact forms for appointment bookings and forms it is a multiconcept and multipurpose theme. Demo: http://sktthemesdemo.net/pathwaypro/
