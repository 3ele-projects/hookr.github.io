Version 1.0 – One of the the best designs from WpThemeGenerator, this theme, is minimalistic and adaptable. Take advantage of it using all the custom post types it brings, Products, Portfolio, Gallery and Testimonials. Shipped with a powerful shopping cart, it is easy for you to show and sell your products. With its powerful backend framework, you can customize layouts, update slider images, update logo, change layout styles and much more.
