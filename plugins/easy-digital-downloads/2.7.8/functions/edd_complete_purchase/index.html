edd_complete_purchase

Complete a purchase.

Description

edd_complete_purchase( (int) $payment_id, (string) $new_status, (string) $old_status ); 

Performs all necessary actions to complete a purchase. Triggered by the edd_update_payment_status(…) function.

Parameters (3)

0. $payment_id (int)
The ID number of the payment
1. $new_status (string)
The status of the payment, probably publish
2. $old_status (string)
The status of the payment prior to being marked as complete,, probably pending
