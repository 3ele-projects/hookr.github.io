ass_group_notification_forum_posts

When a new forum topic or post is posted in bbPress, either: 1) Send emails to all group subscribers 2) Prepares to record it for digest purposes - see {@link ass_group_forum_record_digest()}.

Description

ass_group_notification_forum_posts( $post_id ); 

Hooks into the bbPress action - bb_new_post - to easily identify new forum posts vs edits.

Parameters (1)

0. $post_id
The post id.
