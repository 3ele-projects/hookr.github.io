XML Google Maps By Patrick Matusz

Version 1.9 – This plugin allows you to easily insert Google Map or Google Earth Plugin Maps into your blog. Supports KML, KMZ, GPX, geoRSS filelinks. Supports Dfeault, Satellite, Hybrid, Physical and Google Earth Plugin Maptypes By Patrick Matusz.
