WooCommerce Compare Products LITE By a3rev Software

Version 2.5.4 – Compare Products uses your existing WooCommerce Product Categories and Product Attributes to create Compare Product Features for all your products. A sidebar Compare basket is created that users add products to and view the Comparison in a Compare this pop-up screen. By a3rev Software.
